# Content Architecture – SEO Structure

## Overview

The content strategy is designed using a **Pillar + Cluster SEO framework**, which helps search engines understand the depth and relevance of the website.

---

## Pillar Page

Topic:
Best Coaching Center in Vijayawada for SSC, Intermediate & IIT-JEE/NEET

Purpose:
- Target high-intent keyword
- Act as main conversion page
- Link to all supporting content

---

## Supporting Clusters

### 1. SSC Coaching in Vijayawada

Focus: School-level students (Classes 6–10)

---

### 2. Intermediate Coaching in Vijayawada

Focus: MPC & BiPC students

---

### 3. IIT-JEE / NEET Coaching in Vijayawada

Focus: Competitive exam aspirants

---

### 4. How to Choose the Right Coaching Institute

Focus: Decision-making guidance for students & parents

---

## Internal Linking Strategy

- Each cluster blog links back to the pillar page
- Pillar page links to all clusters
- Cross-linking between related blogs

---

## SEO Benefits

- Improves topical authority
- Helps Google understand content hierarchy
- Increases time spent on website
- Boosts ranking potential

---

## Conversion Flow

Blog → Pillar Page → CTA → Enquiry

This ensures that content not only attracts traffic but also converts visitors into potential students.
